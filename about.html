About Us!

Who are we?


We are a non-profit organisation in Singapore run solely by volunteers in their own free time. Our goal is to raise awareness of the suffering of foreign workers in Singapore and to better their lives, by better integrating them into Singaporean society and educating the Singaporean population on the topic of foreign workers.

We welcome everyone who is willing to help further our cause and are always looking for new enthusiastic volunteers.
